Climate Change Causes & Mitigation

(Updated on Sep. 2022)

Motivated by a strong interest in environmental sustainability, this research analyzes the relationship between global temperature change and energy consumption patterns. The analysis correlates global temperature data with energy consumption across various fuel types, identifying key emission contributors and exploring the role of renewable energy sources, particularly wind power, in global energy transition strategies.
